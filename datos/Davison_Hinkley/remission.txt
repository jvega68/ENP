Cancer Remission and Cell Activity

SUMMARY:
       The remission data frame has 27 rows and 3 columns.

DATA DESCRIPTION:
       This data frame contains the following columns:

LI:    A measure of cell activity.
m:     The number of patients  in  each  group  (all  values  are
       actually 1 here).
r:     The number of patients (out of m) who went into remission.

SOURCE:
       The data were obtained from

       Freeman, D.H. (1987) Applied  Categorical  Data  Analysis.
       Marcel Dekker.

OTHER REFERENCES:
       Davison, A.C. and Hinkley, D.V. (1997)  Bootstrap  Methods
       and Their Application. Cambridge University Press.
