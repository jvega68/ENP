Number of Flaws in Cloth

SUMMARY:
       The cloth data frame has 32 rows and 2 columns.

DATA DESCRIPTION:
       This data frame contains the following columns:
x:     The length of the roll of cloth.
y:     The number of flaws found in the roll.

SOURCE:
       The data were obtained from

       Bissell,  A.F.  (1972)  A  negative  binomial  model  with
       varying element size.  Biometrika, 59, 435-441.

OTHER REFERENCES:
       Davison, A.C. and Hinkley, D.V. (1997)  Bootstrap  Methods
       and Their Application. Cambridge University Press.
